Best Movies of all time according to Ganesh Yadava

My top 3 favourite movies

Avengers: Infinity War

Directed by: Russo brothers
Cast: Robert Downey Jr, Chris Evans, Mark Ruffalo

Its a movie full of crazy superhero action sequences. A movie close to my heart

Inception

Directed by: Christopher Nolan
Cast: Leonardo Dicaprio, Tom Hardy, Cillian Murphy

Absolutely mind bending film. And the ending leaves you thinking for days. Nolan's masterpiece

RRR

Directed by: S.S.Rajamouli
Cast: NTR Jr, Ram Charan, Olivia Morris

Best movie ever to come from India. It was one of the biggest cinematic event in India. Visual Extravaganza.
